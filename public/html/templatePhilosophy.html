Tapping the rich, cost-free well of words

by Kai Lavatai

Don't go out and buy a thesaurus.

Words will not make you sound smarter as much as they will make you seem funner, truer and more real. Words describe our world. They define it. It could be thought of like this: The greater the variety of words, the higher life's resoltuion. Words don't need to be big or fancy but they need to cut straight through the bullshit.

If you need to use more of them in a single sentence, use more of them, but a brief ideally crafted knuckle sandwich will suffice.

Word salad can be an attraction. We can draw with words rather than write with them. It isn't necessary to mean something linguistically to hold meaning visually.
